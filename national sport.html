Sumo wrestling

In Japan, sumo wrestling was under Imperial patronage between 710 and 1185 and was a popular spectator sport./p

During this era it was refined from a brutal submission spectacle into a highly ritualized toppling match in which victory could be gained by forcing the opponent out of a 15-foot circle./p
